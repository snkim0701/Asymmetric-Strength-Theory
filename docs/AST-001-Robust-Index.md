# Absorption: 
Volatility alone does not indicate weakness. High volatility may arise because both buying pressure and selling pressure 
are simultaneously strong. In such a market, the key question is not how large the volatility is, but whether selling 
pressure is absorbed by latent buying demand. We define this property as Absorption.

# HP_2 (Expectation-Absorption Hypothesis)

A larger positive gap between analyst consensus target price and the current market price increases latent buying demand. Therefore, stocks with greater upside potential are expected to exhibit stronger downside absorption during market corrections.
